Aditya Anantharaman

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Computational Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University, PA.

Previously, I worked as a Research Intern at the Visual Learning and Intelligence (VIGIL) Lab at Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad, India under Dr. Vineeth Balasubramanian. Here, I worked on a novel multi-space approach to Zero-Shot Object Detection.

In the summer of 2018, I interned at Microsoft (R&D), Hyderabad, India in the Azure Networking Team.

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, India.

My interests are broadly in machine learning, natural language processing and computer vision. I have worked on problems in the domains of object detection, semi-supervised learning, paraphrase detection, medical imaging, image retrieval and data mining.
